Stew

Description

When it comes to making a traditional Irish Stew, there is no special recipe.

It’s a basic dish with few ingredients that easy to prepare and it tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions
  • 4 large Carrots
  • ½ stewing steak/mince or lamb
  • 8 large potatoes
  • Salt & pepper (personal preference)

Steps

  1. Wash and cut onions into moderate size chunks and add to the pot. (don’t like onions? Do not worry, this can still be cooked without onions).
  2. Wash carrots and cut into chunks (some prefer thicker chunks for taste).
  3. Wash and peel the potatoes (spuds) then cut each one in half.
  4. Slice the meat into smaller pieces. If using stewing mince roll into meatballs.
  5. Get a large pot and fill it with water. Add the potatoes and meat.
